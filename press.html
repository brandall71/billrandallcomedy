Biography

Bill Randall is a stand-up comedian from Wilkes-Barre, Pennsylvania. Known for his sharp observational humor and relatable takes on everyday life, Bill has performed at venues across the region including Wisecrackers Comedy Club and the Good Karma Comedy Fest.

A semi-finalist in NEPA's Funniest competition, Bill brings an honest, self-deprecating style to the stage — the kind that makes audiences laugh while nodding along. Husband, dad, and comedian trying to eat better. Mostly failing at that last part.
